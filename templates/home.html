Global warming today is mainly caused by the accumulation of greenhouse gases in the atmosphere, one of these being extensive carbon dioxide emissions from the continuous production of plastics, synthetic fabrics, and other materials. People today often buy items without full usage of the products, resulting in a wastage of the materials previously stated. For this reason, the donation of unused or only very slightly used items to others would significantly reduce material production, and thereby lower carbon emissions into the atmosphere. This website aims to organize a system through which people can donate and buy items free of cost.
